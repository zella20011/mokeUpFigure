Affiliate Program

We are integrating with local game and comic book shops into our business model, opening a whole new line of products for stores, and helping you bridge the gap between the online and in-person customer base.

Furthermore, we invite your store to join our affiliate program. As an affiliated game store, every purchase made on our in-store tablet, or kiosk, we revenue share. In fact, we are such big believers in the game store experience, we are willing to take on all shipping expenses if a customer is willing to picking up their miniatures from your store.

Other benefits include:

  • Access to custom items available only in your store (i.e. Miniature’s items likes shields or capes with store logo)
  • Special bases that can only be purchased from your store
  • STL Options
  • Management Control
  • No Inventory
  • Hassle-free and addition revenue source
  • Most importantly, no upfront costs
